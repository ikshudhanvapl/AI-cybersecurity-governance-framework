## Notes on Citations

**Format:** All citations follow the Chicago Manual of Style (17th edition), notes-bibliography system, as appropriate for professional policy documents.

**Access:** Where authoritative documents are publicly accessible, URLs are provided. Documents available only through institutional access (e.g., ISO standards) are cited without URL.

**Currency:** Regulatory disclosure timelines are accurate as of April 2026. Regulatory requirements are subject to change; organisations implementing this framework should verify current obligations with qualified legal counsel.

**Proposed additions:** To propose a new reference for inclusion in a future framework version, open an issue using the [Framework Amendment template](../.github/ISSUE_TEMPLATE/framework-amendment.md) and include the full Chicago-format citation and the section(s) to which it is relevant.
